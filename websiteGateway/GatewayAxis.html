Customization journey.

Axis is not a one-size-fits-all tracker. The product can be configured around the places and alerts that matter most to each family.

Customer Inquiry

The customer discovers Axis through the website and reaches out to discuss their tracking needs.

Consultation

We identify the family member, daily routes, destination types, and the places that require monitoring.

Alert Areas

The customer selects zones such as school, university, hospital, workplace, home, or restricted areas.

Excel Mapping

Each area is organized with its coordinates, zone type, assigned color, and alert behavior.

Geofence Design

Rectangular fences are created around the selected locations and prepared for app integration.

App Delivery

The Axis app is customized with the customer’s geofences, colors, and notification rules.
